Founder of the french metal band NightShade, originally from Brest (FR), now living in Tokyo (JP), he already toured in almost the entire world (Europe, USA, China, Japan…), got his records to hit top 10 charts, music in many video games etc…


With his new project, he’s mixing his metal universe with his EDM and hiphop influences, to deliver some killer tracks, at the same time melodic and brutal. Collaborating with artists such as Chunk! No, Captain Chunk! (FR), Conquer Divide (USA), Saosin (USA), Sho Sensei (JP) and many more, already gaining a place in the EDM industry, got the chance to play at the biggest Japanese club « Ageha », having more than 30k monthly listeners on Spotify only, getting exposure in media such as Wide Future, endorsed by Rockstar Energy Drink, Creve Clothing, Killstar Clothing…


DVRK is showing the world that he’s having a bright future coming for him., you’ve been warned!
